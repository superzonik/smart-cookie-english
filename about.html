About Us

Smart Cookie English is an online language school based in the Philippines.

          SmartCookie English is an Online Language School that aims to bring significant improvement in the lives of both it's valued students, focusing on English language growth, academic and social skills, moreover the school aims to hone a universally competitive and positively confident individual that is ready to take on the challenges of the world through the various courses that we offer and with the help of our trustworthy English language specialists.

We specialize in the following English Courses depending on your needs!

  • Practical English as a Second Language (ESL) training courses
  • Test of English for International Communication (TOEIC)
  • International English Language Testing System (IELTS)
  • Business English courses

We welcome everyone from all over the world and we ensure to produce confident and competive students and individuals through our quality and stringent educational standards.

Get ready for the world! Learn naturally, speak naturally.

Vision:

  1. We provide quality, measurable and time bounded lessons to students while always taking into consideration each of our student's unique learning styles, pacing or limitations.

  2. We are one with our students in realizing that they have to take ownership and responsibility of their education by “always keeping the fire burning” within them so they could take hold of their goals in improving their overall English language skills. In which they are always encourage to actively attend and participate in their classes, become inquisitive and do not limit themselves with what they can achieve.

  3. We provide relevant and top of the line class materials, books or files for various courses that will enable students to unleash their full potential in learning English. Our energetic and compassionate English specialists will do to the best of their abilities to mold students to know how to “learn English by heart”.

  4. For our young learners, we will foster a school culture based on a strong and effective partnership between our administration, teachers, parents, and students themselves.

Values:

  1. SmartCookie English being founded in the Philippines will keep the balance of being both student and teacher centered for we believe that great learning comes when educators and learners are given equal rights and appreciation of their efforts in a non judgmental and positively encouraging environment.

  2. SmartCookie English is a licensed and registered online English School based in the Philippines with the aim of teaching the English language. The school will provide various courses meant to teach basic to intensive English language level.

Client Market

          We take pride in creating and developing outstanding relationship with customers. We understand that there is a need for non native English speakers to hone their ability to use the English in the field of business, job application interview, socialization and even travel and leisure. Our target clients will be very diverse, relating to different countries, speaking different languages.
